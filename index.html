Innovation via collaboration

I can handle most every aspect of your digital project, from prototype to product. But when something can be done faster and cheaper by another local developer or designer, I'm willing to work with them to save you money and get your project finished faster. I won't lie: I'm pretty good at most digital things, but I'm not superman. Nor do I feel threatened by the idea of other developers working with 'my' client; I want you to get the best bang for your buck. I also want to collaborate with you, the client, who I view more as a partner than a customer. Your success is my success. Plain and simple.

Transparency-first

Big agencies almost always require you to pay a hefty retainer fee before they even start work on your project. Then they slap you with an exorbitant final fee based upon billable hours. I do not operate on a billable hours fee schedule. Instead, I assess the approximate cost and time needed for your project before we meet to discuss my proposal, let you know the cost on-the-spot, and then leave it up to you to decide whether it's reasonable before signing any contracts. I also endeavor to keep every receipt and track every cost incurred to provide you proof and peace of mind that you're not paying more than you should.

Locally-sourced, locally-minded

I'm based in Savannah, Ga., and think all great things start out local. As such, all locally-owned small businesses (less than 10 employees) will automatically receive 15 percent off services, meaning in most cases you'd be paying almost zero surcharge to me. Sure, I might make less income in the short-run by doing this, but I'm passionate about helping local efforts, and it's worth it to me in the long run to make less money immediately.
